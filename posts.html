🦈 Sharks are Older Than Saturn's Rings

Sharks have lived on Earth for over 400 million years, while Saturn's rings formed only about 100 million years ago.

🐙 Octopus Power: 3 Hearts!

Did you know an octopus has three hearts? Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while one pumps blood to the rest of the body. That's one reason octopuses are such incredible sea creatures!
